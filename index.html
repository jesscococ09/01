Welcome to the World of Telemedicine

In this site you will learn about how telemedicine uses technology to support healthcare from a distance. You’ll explore what telemedicine is, how it connects to computer science, the different ways it can be delivered, and the general advantages and challenges that come with it. The goal is to give you a clear, simple starting point for understanding how modern tools are shaping the way people receive care today.
